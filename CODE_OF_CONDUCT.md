# Code of Conduct

We are committed to creating a respectful, safe, and productive environment for everyone who participates in this project.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussions professional and on topic.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Respect privacy and do not share confidential information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks, threats, or doxxing.
- Sharing private code, secrets, or user data.
- Spam, trolling, or disruptive behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and direct communications related to the project.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove content or restrict access for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the maintainer via the email listed in the README.
